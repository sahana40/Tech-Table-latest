About Us

TechTable is an Ed-Tech Startup by the Students of IITM which aims to connect students to opportunity. We are building the Ecosystem for Students and Startups to reduce the gap between academics and project-based learning.

Startup Connect

It is a platform to connect Engineering students and start-ups. Get connected to the best start-ups around India through this platform. It is always a win-win to situation for both during the internship. As students get the hands-on experience and Industries get people to work in innovative ways.
